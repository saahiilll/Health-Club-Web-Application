About Us

This Health Club operated by H.V.P.M. Hanuman Vyayam Prasarak Mandal popularly known as H.V.P.M.is a premier institute of physical education in India. It is located in Maharashtra. In Amravati city. It is situated near Ambadevi temple. It is well known for its sports activities. It was set up by Mr. Anant Vaidya and Mr. Ambadas Vaidya around 1914. Previously it was known as Hanuman Akhada.
